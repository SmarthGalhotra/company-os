# Skill: 1on1-prep

Prepare for an accelerator or team 1-on-1, and capture follow-ups after.

## Before the call
1. Pull context: who you're meeting, last interaction, open items you owe them, relevant KPIs.
2. Produce a one-page brief: their likely agenda, your asks, 3 questions worth asking, open follow-ups.

## After the call
1. From notes/transcript, extract decisions, commitments (yours and theirs), and intros promised.
2. Create Trello cards for each commitment with owner + date (auto, per `POLICY.md`).
3. Draft any promised intro emails — but hold for approval (leaves the company).

## Output
A pre-call brief, and post-call a set of tracked follow-ups so nothing promised gets dropped.
