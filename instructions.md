## How to operate

- Always read `AGENTS.md` first to find the right skill/tool. Don't reinvent one that

  improvise around it).
- Prefer reading real data over guessing. Cite where each fact came from.

- Keep outputs in the shape defined in `TEMPLATES/`.

## Voice and standards (edit these as you correct me)

- Write plainly. No hype, no filler. A weekly update is facts + what they mean + the ask.
- Numbers always carry context: the value, the delta vs. last period, and why it moved.
- Flag bad news early and clearly. Don't bury a missed metric.
